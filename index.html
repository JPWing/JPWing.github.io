Paddle Star
TARGET RANGE: 800
COST: 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 Mana
COOLDOWN: 7.5 / 7 / 6.75 / 6.5 / 6.25

First Cast: Zoe hurls a star in the target direction that collides with the first enemy hit, dealing magic damage in a small area. The damage is based both on champion level and ability rank, and is increased by 0% - 150% (based on distance traveled).

If the star does not hit an enemy, it lingers at maximum range for 1 second.

While the star is airborne or while idle, Zoe can reactivate Paddle Star to redirect the star to a new location near her, resetting its damage modifier on cast. If Zoe moves with the redirected star, it will keep moving until it moves more than 800 units away from her.

Both the initial cast and reactivation will prime More Sparkles!.

Spell Thief
COOLDOWN: 0.25

Passive - Spell Thief: Whenever enemy champions cast a summoner spell or item active, they drop the corresponding Spell Shard onto the ground that grant  sight over a 100-radius area. Spell Shards last for 40 seconds. Some enemy minions spawn with random Spell Shards that only drop to the ground for 20 seconds if killed by Zoe.

Collecting a Spell Shard allows Zoe to activate Spell Thief within the next 60 seconds. She can replace her current Spell Thief active by issuing a move command directly on another Spell Shard.

Active: Zoe mimics the active of the collected Spell Shard.

Passive - Wheeeee: Casting Spell Thief or one of her own summoner spells grants Zoe bonus movement speed as well as summoning three bubbles that orbit her for the next 10 seconds. The bubbles autonomously hurl themselves at the nearest enemy, prioritising her attack target, dealing magic damage.

Sleepy Trouble Bubble
TARGET RANGE: 800
EFFECT RADIUS: 250
COST: 80 Mana
COOLDOWN: 16 / 15.5 / 15 / 14.5 / 14

Active: Zoe kicks a ball in the target direction that bursts on the first enemy hit, else forming a trap at maximum range for 4 seconds that arms after a brief delay. The ball can only move through terrain once but may do so indefinitely, can only move up to 650-units once outside terrain, and will fall short if it would enter terrain again.

The ball deals magic damage on contact with an enemy and renders them  drowsy for 2.2 seconds, which gradually  slows them, after which they fall  asleep for 2 seconds.

COST: 50 Mana
COOLDOWN: 11 / 8 / 5

Active: Zoe targets a location and then dives into a portal that she opens beneath her, blinking to the target location after a very brief delay. After 1 second, she falls back through the portal and blinks back to her cast location.

Zoe remains able to attack and cast abilities during Portal Jump, and can  see over walls, but cannot move.
